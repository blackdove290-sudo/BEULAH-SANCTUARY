Who We Are

Led by Faith, Rooted in Community

Worldwide Gospel Churches Of Kenya

Beulah Sanctuary Mombasa is a vibrant family of believers located in Mishomoroni. We are dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ throughout the Coastal region and beyond.

Our motto reflects our heart: "A place where everybody is somebody and a place where God dwells." We are committed to sound biblical teaching, authentic worship, and serving our community with the love of Christ.
